The Story

Why I Started
Gaskey Solutions

I'm Samuel Gaskey — an 18-year-old Marketing Arts student who got tired of watching small businesses get ripped off by agencies charging thousands of dollars for results they could get for a fraction of the price.

I started Gaskey Solutions because I know the tools. I understand the platforms. And I saw an opportunity to do it better, faster, and cheaper than the bloated agencies that dominate the market.

Every project I take on gets my full personal attention — no account managers passing messages, no junior designers making decisions. You work directly with me, and I work directly on your brand.

My background in Marketing Arts gives me a foundation in strategy, consumer psychology, and brand storytelling. But it's the hands-on work — building sites, running campaigns, analyzing data — that gives me an edge.

What I Stand For
